Treason

With great actors and artists embracing new concepts to developed traditional tango, this intense and vibrant production captures the essence of the Argentine Tango cosmogony through its music and aesthetic and by the elegance of its form. This story can happen in any country, which generates empathy with the characters, but the magnificent neighborhood of La Boca is the natural setting to reveal the intensity of life in Buenos Aires with its unique characteristics.

Secret Tango Stories was born in the UK in collaboration between Santiago and Gerald, forged to reflect the authenticity of the Argentinian Tango through its music and aesthetics. With original soundtracks composed with the same old passion, STS creates new synergies between countries and cultures, reflecting on the screen, with great quality and style.



Our mission is to spread Tango worldwide

That's why we are already working on our next episodes

Destiny

In a small farm in Wales, John loses his wife. With little Sara, he moves to the ity where she grows becoming a great
classical dance student like her mother. A trip to the Colon Theater in Buenos Aires changes everything in their lives.

Pasion

Andy has runoff from his farm town because of police matters. In the city, he meets his uncle that works in a Tango Club. When Jessica, a jewess girl, arrives at the milonga, the passion takes them to serious trouble with everyone.

Exile

Paula is a Tango dancer that works at the square with his partner and at little milongas in Buenos Aires. Ahmed is a nigerian exiled that sells glasses in the main train station. When their lives cross new passions are unleashed.
